Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. Probably.

How long does it take to install a pool?
Pool installation typically takes 6 to 8 weeks, depending on the size, complexity, soil conditions, weather, staff availability, material delivery delays, and whether Bob is on leave.
How much does a pool cost?
Prices start from R85,000 for a basic pool and can go up significantly depending on your choices. Please contact us for a quote. The quote is free but the pool is not.
Do you offer a guarantee on your work?
Yes! We offer a comprehensive 5-year structural guarantee and a 90-day workmanship guarantee. These are different things. Please ask us to explain the difference before signing anything.
What areas do you service?
We service all of Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas. For areas outside of this, please contact us and we'll let you know if Bob feels like driving that far.
Can I see examples of your previous work?
Absolutely! Our portfolio is available on request. Our website gallery is currently being updated and has been since 2022. We have photos on our phone if you come in person.
